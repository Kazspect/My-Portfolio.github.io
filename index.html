About Me

Ohayo! I'm Kazspect, an aspiring game developer with a passion for creating fun and engaging experiences. I enjoy bringing the minds wildest ideas to life through gameplay, animations, environments, programming and other creative designs.

Using programs such as Unity, Unreal Engine, Krita, Maya, and Blender; I've developed a variety of projects that have helped me grow my skills within the industry. Each project is an opportunity to learn something new and create experiences for players to enjoy through environmental storytelling, gameplay, UI elements, and story.

I'm always looking to improve my craft and widen my skillset, explore new ideas, and continue my journey as a game developer.
